About SANBS

The South African National Blood Service (SANBS) is a non-profit organisation committed to providing a safe and sufficient blood supply to patients across South Africa.

Our Vision

To be the cornerstone of healthcare through the provision of affordable and reliable blood services.

What We Do

  • Collect and test blood donations
  • Ensure blood product safety and availability
  • Conduct donor education and recruitment
  • Support hospitals and emergency services
