Why Donate Blood?

Your donation can save lives — it's that simple.

Every Drop Counts

Blood is needed every day for emergencies, surgeries, cancer treatments, and chronic illnesses. Just one donation can save up to three lives.

Who Needs Blood?

  • Accident victims
  • Patients undergoing surgery
  • Cancer patients
  • Women with pregnancy complications
  • Children with anemia or other disorders

How Often Can I Donate?

Healthy individuals can donate whole blood every 56 days (about 8 weeks). Platelet and plasma donations may have different guidelines.

Benefits of Donating

  • Free health screening with every donation
  • Satisfaction of helping others
  • Improves iron regulation in some donors
  • Community recognition

It’s Quick and Easy

The entire process takes about 30–45 minutes. Most people say it’s painless and rewarding.
