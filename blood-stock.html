Current Blood Stock Levels

Blood levels are monitored daily to ensure we can support hospitals across South Africa. Here's the current status:

  • O+ : Available
  • A+ : Available
  • B+ : Low
  • AB+ : Critical
  • O- : Available
  • A- : Low
  • AB- : Critical

What do these levels mean?

  • Available: Sufficient for current needs.
  • Low: Shortage is possible; donors encouraged.
  • Critical: Urgent need. Immediate donations required.
